My Top 6 Favourite Animes

Favourite Opening Song

This is my all-time favorite anime opening. A couple of years ago, I attended a Candlelight Concert with the theme "Favorite Anime Themes," and it was an unforgettable experience. The songs brought back so many nostalgic memories that I was almost brought to tears. But when "Blue Bird" by Ikimono Gakari started playing, my friends and nearly screamed and jumped out of our seats in pure joy.
